This is my corner of the internet. I'm Daria — a senior software engineer with 20+ years across QA, development, business analysis, and project management. I'm currently at nib Group in Newcastle, Australia, where I work on digital platforms.

I started this blog because I have things to say about the intersection of software engineering, AI, and the human judgement that ties them together. The industry is changing fast, and I want to think out loud about what that means for the people doing the work.

Expect posts about:

- **AI and software engineering** — how the craft is evolving, what's hype, what's real
- **The specification bottleneck** — why knowing *what* to build matters more than ever
- **Career positioning** — navigating change with experience, not panic
- **Lessons from both sides** — having been both the person writing the code and the person writing the requirements gives me a perspective I don't see discussed enough

No posting schedule, no content strategy. Just writing when something's worth saying.
